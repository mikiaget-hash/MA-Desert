Our Story

A little house
with big flavors

MA Desert House started as a dream in a small home kitchen. Mira Dael — baker, barista, and forever cake enthusiast — wanted a place where neighbors could gather over something sweet and a really good cup of coffee.

Every cake is still made by hand, every macchiato pulled with care. Nothing industrial, nothing rushed. Just honest, delicious food made from ingredients we actually believe in.
